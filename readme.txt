Let your visitors share your products via mail.

== Description ==
Your visitors will be able to share your rpoducts via email. Increase your readership/members and followers.
You will be able to set link backs and signatures on the emails being sent.

If any issues regarding this plugin mail me @: sandeep.tete2013@gmail.com


== Installation ==
- Upload zip file.
- Activate the plugin.
- Scroll down to Share Woocommerce Email through dashboard.
- Modify the settings.
- Done.
